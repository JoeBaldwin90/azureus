"We all acted naturally"

The mysterious center of all things

A treasure for those who are good. A refuse for those who are not.

Beatiful words can be traded, noble deeds can enhance reputations. But if people lack them, why should they be rejected?

Nothing can compare to the stillness. Honouring the way.

Didn't they say; Through it seekers find, through it the guilty escape?

Great rising and falling

People only know it exists. Next they see and praise. Soon they fear. Finally they depise.

Without fundamental trust, there is no trust at all.

Be careful in valuing words. Whenthe work is done, everyone says; we just acted naturally.

Humans

Are born soft and weak. They die stiff and strong.

Ten thousand plants and trees are born soft and tender. And die withered and sere.

The stiff and strong are death's companions. The soft and weak are life's companions.

Therefore,

The strongest armies do not conquer. The greatest trees are cut down. The strong and great sink down. The soft and weak rise up.
